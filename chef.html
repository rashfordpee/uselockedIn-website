Hey there! I'm The Jiggy Designer

Welcome to my digital kitchen! I'm a passionate developer and designer who believes that productivity tools should be as delightful as they are functional. Just like a chef crafts the perfect recipe, I craft digital experiences that help people stay focused and achieve their goals.

The idea for LockedIn came from my own struggles with digital distractions. As someone who spends countless hours in front of a screen, I found myself constantly battling the urge to check AI tools, social media, and endless rabbit holes of information. I realized I needed a solution that was both powerful and simple ‚Äì something that would keep me "locked in" to what truly mattered.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me experimenting with new productivity techniques, reading about cognitive psychology, or actually cooking real food in my real kitchen! I believe that the best digital tools are those that help us live better lives offline.

LockedIn represents my philosophy: focus is a superpower, and everyone deserves access to tools that help them harness it. Whether you're a student cramming for exams, a developer debugging complex code, or a creative working on your next masterpiece, LockedIn is here to help you stay in your zone.

Why I Built LockedIn

In our hyper-connected world, maintaining focus has become a rare skill. I wanted to create something that would help people reclaim their attention and direct it toward meaningful work. LockedIn isn't just another productivity app ‚Äì it's a commitment to intentional living in the digital age.

Every feature in LockedIn has been carefully crafted based on research in cognitive science and my own experience as someone who struggles with digital distractions. The reflection journaling, the customizable blocking, the focus sessions ‚Äì they all work together to create a holistic approach to digital wellness.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Your feedback and support mean the world to me, and I'm constantly working to make LockedIn better. Together, we're building a more focused, intentional digital world.
