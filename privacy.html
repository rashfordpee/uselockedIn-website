Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Introduction

LockedIn ("we", "us", or "our") operates the LockedIn browser extension (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Fundamental Principle: Your data belongs to you. We collect the minimum necessary information to provide our service, and we never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

How We Store Your Information

All your data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.local API:

  • Session data is automatically deleted when your session ends
  • Reflection journals remain stored until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data
  • No data is ever transmitted to external servers

Local Storage Only: Your data never leaves your computer. We don't have servers that store your information, so you remain in complete control.
