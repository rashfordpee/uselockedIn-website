WHAT IS LOCKED IN??

LockedIn is a powerful Chrome extension designed to help you maintain laser focus during your work sessions. By blocking distracting websites and providing structured focus sessions, LockedIn ensures you stay productive and engaged with your tasks.

With built-in journaling features for reflection, LockedIn becomes your personal productivity coach, helping you build better work habits and achieve your goals.
